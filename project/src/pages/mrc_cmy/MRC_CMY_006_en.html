About Us

Employee Benefits

  • Total well-being
  • Caring for your family
  • Work-life balance
  • Career development support
  • Developer centric culture

Total well-being

  • Physical exam
    We care for you and your spouse’s health. You can have a paid day off so that you and your spouse can take physical exam at the partner medical centers nearby.
  • Comprehensive health insurance
    Getting sick? We support medical expenses of you, your spouse and children.
  • Infertility support
    We provide infertility treatment expenses for you and your spouse, and infertility leave that can be used for total of 3 times within 1 year.
  • On-site counseling center
    Have any matters you can’t talk about? Do not worry! Our professional counselors are always on hand to help you find inner peace through counseling.
  • On-site clinic
    Getting sick at work? You can get medical care by a doctor from Samsung Medical Center at the on-site clinic.
  • On-site fitness center
    Always stay fit and revitalize yourself by exercising at the on-site fitness center operated by Hotel Shilla!
  • Pension subsidy
    We subsidize 50% of your pension to prepare better for the era of homo hundred!

Caring for your family

  • Congratulations and condolences supplements
    Sharing happiness makes it double and sharing sadness cuts it in half. To express congratulations or condolences, we provide congratulatory and condolence wreaths, funeral directors and helpers, items and money.
  • Congratulations and condolences support for retirees
    When you retire after more than 10 years of service, the company supports you, your spouse, and your children for 10 years on the congratulatory/condolatory occasion.
  • Newborn gifts
    Congratulations on the birth of new baby! We provide newborn gifts for every childbirth.
  • Tuition assistance
    The education of your children matters to us! Your children can focus on studying with our tuition assistance program from kindergarten to university.
  • Elementary school supplies package
    We provide school supplies when a child first enter the elementary school to cheer up working parents. Seeing your child happy makes you happy!

Work-life balance

  • Corporate discounts at resorts
    Having troubles when booking holiday accommodations? You can get corporate discount at luxury resorts at the most popular sites in the nation.
  • Corporate discounts at “Caribbiean Bay”(water park)
    Live, cool and cheap refresh! We provide the Caribbean Bay Water Park discount tickets.
  • Everland admission tickets
    Explore the realm of dreams and fantasies with your family! We offer Everland admission tickets to support Miracom employees' refreshment. Enjoy the freedom to visit Everland at your leisure.
  • Long service leave
    Take a rest after hard work! You can take long service leave after a period of 10,15,20,25,30,40 years’ continuous service.
  • Selective Benefits
    We provide welfare points, which can be used on/off-line variably according to the individual needs such as self-improvements, leisure and health etc.
  • Employees leisure clubs
    Join various leisure clubs, enjoy activities and get to know colleagues better!

Career development support

  • Education & training
    Do you want to upgrade yourself in various fields? Go for it! We support you to be an expert on IT, foreign languages, nosiness skills and so on!
  • Certification exam support
    Be a better me than yesterday! We support certificate exam/renewal fees to catch up new IT technologies and trends.
  • Mentoring program
    We have a mentoring program to provide a soft landing for new hires.

Developer centric culture

  • Flexible working hours
    Working and playing are equally important. You can adjust working hours to suit your plan.
  • Housing finance
    Housing expenses are soaring. We support housing finance interest if you work or house location is farther than Chungcheongnam-do.
  • Relocation assistance
    Has your workplace changed? We subsidize relocation expenses.
  • Homecoming expense
    Missing home and family? We support homecoming expenses if you work at long distance project’s site of if your work location is farther than Chungcheongnam-do.
  • On-site food court
    A healthy lunch break at work makes you happier! Enjoy selections of healthy and delicious foods served by professional chefs, Three times a day!
