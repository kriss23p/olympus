Be Smart with Smart Factory.
Miracom has been a game changer spearheading the intelligent manufacturing industry for the past 25 years. We have the best-in-class smart factory professionals who spare no effort in providing a one-stop service from consulting to operation utilizing 20-year-long know-how and expertise.
For decades, we have pursued co-growth and development on the back of deep-seated trust with our partners and clients. We pull out all the stops to provide state-of-the-art technology and services for manufacturing competitiveness that brings end-to-end satisfaction for customers and their customers.

Core Values
These values are at the heart of every decision Miracom makes.
  • Professional
    Go the extra mile for excellence.

    We are professionals striving for the best products and services on the market. With pride and commitment, we make every effort for excellence.

  • Convergence
    Cross the boundaries to create new values.

    Centering on IT Technology, we cross the boundaries of everything - industries, cultures, generations and so on. We combine the strengths of existing and new things to create something better.

  • Innovation
    Never stay comfortable, always pursue new ideas.

    We explore the open world of challenges and opportunities to bring positive changes for everybody. We consider failure as a process of change, not something to fear or blame.

  • Communication
    Communicate with respect and sincerity.

    We respect our people & customers and listen carefully to opinions and ideas from different points of view. We will share ideas, overcome obstacles and find the best solutions.

  • Collaboration
    Lead, lean on each other and grow together.

    By combining ideas and experiences from diverse teams, we grow and become better together. Open collaboration brings wonderful results.
