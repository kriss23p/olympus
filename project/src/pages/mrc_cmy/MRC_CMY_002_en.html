CEO

Miracom Inc is a leading ICT service provider who supports customer’s digital transformation based on smart factory solutions and digital technology capabilities.

Radical changes are underway in every aspect of our society including the economy, environment and culture. To address these changes, digital transformation based on production and management systems that contain the core of corporate business is becoming more important.

Miracom Inc offers a comprehensive smart factory solution supporting unmanned, automated, and intelligent production systems. By covering everything from deployment to operation of management systems including ERP, we provide total ICT services that enable digital transformation throughout the entire business process.

Based on our extensive industry knowledge and more than 20 years of accumulated technical competence, we take the initiative in providing innovative insights necessary for our customers' growth through digital technology.

We make the best use of proven solutions that we have delivered to over 300 customers, the best practices by industry and function, and create outstanding outcomes that customers can experience through advanced software development capabilities.

As our mission is to ensure our customer’s success, we have secured cutting-edge technologies such as AI, big data analytics, and cloud, and are proactively developing our capabilities so that we can become a trusted partner that grows together with our customers.

Sincerely, Miracom specializes in smart factory solutions that expedite clients’ manufacturing innovation in virtue of smart factory know-how and digital technology-enabled capabilities.

CEO Stanley Kang
