About Us

Job Description

Miracom provides a wide range of training programs designed to nurture task specialists, and even after joining the company; Miracom offers more compatible job selections through proper placements at the right time.

  • Sales/Marketing
  • R&D
  • Consulting
  • Architecture
  • ICT Development/Operation
  • Cloud
  • Quality/Project Management
  • Business Support
  • Product Planning/Management
    Planning, launching, upgrading, and identifying discontinued products based on market analysis and business strategy
  • Marketing
    Establishment and execution of marketing plans based on the market environment and business strategies to enhance the brand value of the company and its products/services and support the development of business opportunities
  • Sales
    To achieve the company’s business goals:
    - Identifying and winning contracts related to the company’s products/services offerings
    - Managing customers and contracts
  • R&D Planning
    Planning and operating Technology to Business by discovering necessary prior technologies to secure business competitive advantage based on technological differentiation
  • R&D
    Securing business competitive advantage based on technological differentiation, researching and turning necessary technologies into SW and providing them to business units
  • Solution Consulting
    Proposal and implementation of optimal solutions on the strength of expertise and prowess to automate and streamline customers’ businesses
  • Data Analytics Consulting
    Analyzing data harnessing industry-specific knowledge and data processing/analytics technology to generate business insights for clients
  • Software Architecture
    Establishing an optimal software development strategy, designing the key software components and their interrelationships, in order to enable stakeholders to collaboratively implement an application/solution with a shared vision.
  • Data Architecture
    Developing optimal data utilization strategy for each client’s business and leading design of key data components, their relationships and database to maintain high quality data
  • Technical Architecture
    Provision of technical standards for cloud infrastructure, hardware, and system-based software Integration of IT infrastructure components, proposal/analysis/design/implementation/optimization of technical architecture for implementation and operation
  • Cloud Architecture
    Securing and standardizing technologies for physical/virtual infrastructure, platforms, and SW to implement cloud products as a service, designing the relationship between each technology element, and leading the architecture so that CMP self-service development can be viable.
  • Industry Specialties
    Creating new business opportunities and support systemization by establishing customer response strategies such as proposing business improvement and introducing new technologies based on industry expertise and information technology (IT) capabilities.
  • Analytical Design
    Designing systems by defining/analyzing/detailing functional and non-functional requirements to provide services that meet customer needs
  • Application Software Development
    Utilizing information technology (IT) and solutions to design and implement application software that meets customer's business requirements and manage quality for stable service
  • Solution Development
    To define, design and develop new solutions based on an understanding of technology and market trends and business requirements
  • Security Technology
    Analyzing security status, identifying security threats, presenting countermeasures, and establishing/building/operating security systems are a must to ensure business continuity not affected by security accidents
  • CX Planning/Design
    Establishing an integrated CX strategy for systems and services to differentiate the experience of goods / services across all contacts between customers and businesses, conduct enterprise CX governance; analyzing/discovering/defining the user's needs and pain points, and planning/designing/designing discriminative use experiences that can solve them
  • System Engineering
    Improving the availability and efficiency of the system by designing, introducing, deploying, operating and improving the cloud infrastructure for providing information system (IT) services.
  • Data Engineering
    Providing stable DB services to customers by optimally configuring customer DB and managing performance and availability
  • Network Engineering
    Performing technical support and proposals for securing network service business, designing and constructing wired and wireless network service, and performing operation management to improve network availability and efficiency
  • Project Management
    Establishing a management plan for project scope, schedule, and resources to ensure customer satisfaction and business profit, and controlling / managing project progress accordingly
  • Quality Management
    Designing a method for setting and realizing quality goals based on the quality characteristics of products and services, and verifying and confirming them according to the plan to ensure quality
  • Planning
    Establishing the vision, mid to long-term strategy and management innovation/digitalization strategy of the company and the independent business unit, review the business feasibility of each business/organization and conducting intracompany coordination
  • Financials
    Providing management information to internal and external stakeholders in a timely manner, and analyzing management performance to support management decision-making for the corporate sustainability
  • Human Resources
    Establishing and operating recruitment, manpower management, human resource development, personnel planning, and personnel support systems for efficient human resource management and management performance improvement according to business strategy
  • Purchase
    Establishing SCM (Supply Chain Management), managing suppliers, information management of supply market/technology/product / outsourcing / price, and securing competitiveness of external resources through negotiation with vendors (Quality, cost, delivery, etc.)
  • Legal Support
    Preventing legal and managerial risks that may arise in the overall business activities such as the management and business of the company, and contributing to the management profit through follow-up management such as issues, disputes
  • Audit
    Performing audit activities on human resources and expenses to create transparent corporate activities and a sound corporate culture in the company
